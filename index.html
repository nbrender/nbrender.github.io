Goals

𐄂 Create BI Center of Excellence.
Create a community among all the data professionals at WE. Provide information, training, and resources on BI best practices. Promote a self-service philosophy.

♦ Update BI Webpage.
♦ Update resources for developers.
♦ Add training resources all around.
♦ Make EA a main site for WE.
♦ Share info via newsletter, video or podcast.
♦ Setup a certification process for BIC reports.




𐄂 Improve Deployment Process.
Move the deployment process to an administrative task run by lower job level or support staff. Automate more of the process to ensure consistency.

♦ Applies to both MSTR and SQL.



Goals

𐄂 Retire Legacy.
Turn off Legacy as a reporting system. In the short term monitor our progress to shifting to Enterprise Analytics.

♦ Create a strategy to cover most used reports.
♦ Measure our progress in data available in EA.
♦ Measure our progress in report usage in EA vs Legacy.




𐄂 Promote Agile Development.
Drive the team to adopt agile development principles with a focus on continual improvement.

♦ Setup regular training to align the team.
♦ Adopt a workflow process that supports what we need.
♦ Continue to think in terms of stories, customer deliverables, and sprints.
♦ Work with our customers to understand what we will be delivering and when.



Goals

𐄂 Determine Optimal Team Size.
Define what skills we need on the team, and then determine which we have and which we do not.

♦ What team roles do we need?
♦ What skillsets do we need?
♦ Who is in those team roles?
♦ What succession plan do we have?
♦ Where does Infocepts and other contractors fit?




𐄂 Support Self-service Reporting.
Provide tools, resources, and a structure that promotes analysts to drive their won reporting and data analysis.

♦ This is closely tied to the COE and most content will be there.
♦ SQL Server setup in a way to support Analysts doing direct data analysis.
♦ MicroStrategy setup to allow Analysts to work directly with data not in cubes in order to build out their own reports or cubes.
♦ Provide training on how to build best in class reports and dashboards.
♦ Provide Analysts with tools to monitor usage and licenses within their divisions.
